What is Kinetis?

Kinetis is a bicycle sharing services platform, allowing everyone to take short trips within the city. Kinetis helps to reduce the traffic congestion, help to reduce environmental pollution, and make the city better place to live in. It serves as a green solution for your short-distance trip. You can hire or return a bike quickly on the App, anytie, everywhere. More detail about Kinetis is coming soon.
